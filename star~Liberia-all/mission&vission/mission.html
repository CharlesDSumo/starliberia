Mission

Our mission is to promote Liberian Music and to help make underground artist to achieve their dream in life. The Star*Liberia is free to all underground artist who want their music to be heard world wide, we also help to write song for underground artist.

Please don't be offended if we keep talking about underground artist, it is simply because this site is build to help mainly underground artist but we can also help professional artist.

Vission

Our Vission is to become one of the well known website around the world that in the next one year our site will have a backend and we will also be able to teach on our site. One of our dream is to become a website that will

teach people how to become a successful musician, we also want Star*Liberia to get members every in part of the world and a good sponsor that will help to support the site.
