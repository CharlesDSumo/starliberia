Brief History Of Star*Liberia

The Star*Liberia was founded on November 16 A.D. 2020 by Charles Determine Sumo. due to my love for Liberian music and underground artist who don't have the money and capacity to promote their dreams, I said to myself there is a need for me to help them to move their dream forward, so that is how I started bulding this site.

At first I never knew where to begin from, with my little knowledge in coding, so I thought it while to visit other site that has already been develop, some of the site I visited were: Tunes Liberia.com and Plus Liberia.com.
